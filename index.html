Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. What types of emergency funds are available?

A. Emergency funds typically include small personal loans, credit card cash advances, government assistance programs (like welfare or unemployment benefits), and payday loans. Each option has different conditions and interest rates, so careful comparison is key.

Q. Can I get a loan with a low credit score?

A. Yes, even with a low credit score, there are options like government-backed programs or specific lenders specializing in subprime loans. Our simulation considers these options when providing recommendations.

Q. Is the repayment simulation accurate?

A. Our simulation is an estimate based on general interest rates and common repayment structures. Actual interest rates, fees, and repayment terms can vary significantly between financial institutions. Always confirm directly with the lender before finalizing any decision.
